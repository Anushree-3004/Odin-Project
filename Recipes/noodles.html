Ingredients

  • 3 ounces of soy sauce-flavored ramen noodles with seasoning packets
  • 2 tablesoops vegetable oil
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 small carrot, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, minced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil

Directions

Step 1

Bring a medium pot of water to a boil.

Cook ramen noodles, reserving seasoning packects, in boiling water until softened, about 3 minutes.

Drain noodles and set aside.

Step 2

Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

Cook and stir green onions in hot oil until softened, 2 to 3 minutes.

Remove green onions to a plate,set aside.

Step 3

Heat 1 teaspoon vegitable oil in the same skillet over medium heat.

Cook and stir carrots, peas and bell pepper seperately until softened, remove each to a plate with green onions when done.
